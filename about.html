About Clash of BaNG

What is Clash of BaNG?

Clash of BaNG is a playful fantasy game community made for players who enjoy troop guides, fan art, clan stories, and beginner-friendly battle tips.

The website is designed to help users explore the world of Clash of BaNG in a simple, friendly, and fun way.

Our Story

Clash of BaNG started as a small online fan space where players shared simple strategies, troop ideas, and creative artwork. Over time, the community grew into a place where beginners and longtime players could learn, explore, and connect with each other.

Today, Clash of BaNG focuses on making game information easier to understand through organised pages, clear navigation, and friendly visual design.

Key Milestones

2018
The Community Started Clash of BaNG began as a small fan forum for players who enjoyed fantasy battles and troop discussions.
2021
Regional Hubs Expanded The community grew into several regional hubs, including Jakarta, Bandung, and Denpasar.
2026
Official Website Project Clash of BaNG launched its website concept to centralise troop guides, fan art, clan registration, and community information.

Creators

Clash of BaNG was created by @mint who wanted to build a friendly space for players to learn and share.

The website focuses on simple navigation, readable content, consistent layout, and playful visual design.
